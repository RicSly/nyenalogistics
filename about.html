Our Journey Begins

Founded in 2020 during a period of rapid business transformation across Africa, Nyena Group was born from a visionary concept: to create an integrated platform that addresses Africa's diverse operational challenges through comprehensive solutions.

Our founder recognized the need for unified business solutions that could seamlessly integrate supply chain, hospitality, real estate, and transportation services. The vision was clear - create a one-stop platform that simplifies business operations across the continent.

Starting with a single service focus, we've strategically expanded to become a pan-African integrated solutions provider operating in 12 countries, serving over 15,000 active clients with a network of 500+ verified partners.

Our Mission

To simplify business operations across Africa by providing integrated solutions that leverage technology, local expertise, and strategic partnerships to overcome operational challenges and drive sustainable economic growth.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted integrated solutions platform by 2030, connecting businesses with efficient, reliable, and comprehensive operational support across all our service areas.

Our Founder & CEO

The visionary leader behind Nyena Logistics' integrated solutions

IY

Issah Yakubu

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 15 years of experience across African markets, Issah Yakubu envisioned and built Nyena Logistics from the ground up. His diverse background in business operations, technology, and African market dynamics provided the unique insight needed to create Africa's premier integrated solutions platform.

Before founding Nyena Logistics, Issah held senior positions in multinational corporations across West Africa within the Maritime space, where he identified the critical need for integrated business solutions that could address the continent's unique operational challenges. His leadership has been instrumental in Nyena Group's expansion across African countries and the development of our four core service areas.

"Our vision at Nyena Logistics is to create seamless business ecosystems that empower African enterprises to thrive. By integrating supply chain, hospitality, real estate, and transportation solutions, we're not just solving problems - we're building the foundation for sustainable economic growth across the continent."
