Our Journey Begins

Founded in 2020 during a period of rapid digital transformation across Africa, Nyena Logistics was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a unified platform that solves the continent's complex logistics challenges.

Our founders, experienced professionals in logistics, technology, and African markets, recognized the fragmentation in Africa's supply chain ecosystem. They saw an opportunity to leverage technology to connect businesses with reliable movement and infrastructure solutions.

Starting with just three team members and a single warehouse in Accra, Ghana, we've grown to become a pan-African platform operating in 12 countries with over 500 verified partners and 15,000+ successful deliveries.

Our Mission

To simplify logistics across Africa by leveraging technology, local expertise, and strategic partnerships to overcome infrastructure challenges and drive economic growth.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted logistics infrastructure platform by 2030, connecting every business to efficient, reliable, and affordable movement solutions.
