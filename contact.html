Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting Nyena Logistics

What are your business hours?

Our standard business hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM GMT. Saturday hours are 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. For urgent matters, we provide 24/7 emergency support through our dedicated hotline: +233 24 123 4567.

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters are prioritized and typically receive a response within 2-4 hours. For emergency situations, please call our 24/7 support line.

Do you have offices in other African countries?

Yes, Nyena Logistics operates in 12+ African countries including Ghana (headquarters), Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ivory Coast, Senegal, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Ethiopia, Zambia, and Mozambique. Contact our headquarters for specific regional office information.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

For the most efficient response, please include: your full name and company, contact information, service interest (logistics, hospitality, shipping, etc.), project scope or requirements, timeline, and any specific challenges you're facing. The more details you provide, the better we can assist you.

Can I schedule an in-person meeting at your office?

Absolutely! We welcome in-person meetings at our headquarters in Accra. Please contact us via phone or email to schedule an appointment. For international clients, we also offer virtual meetings via Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or Google Meet.

Do you offer emergency logistics services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency logistics support for time-sensitive shipments, urgent transportation needs, and critical supply chain situations. Contact our emergency hotline: +233 27 123 4567 for immediate assistance.
