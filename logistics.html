Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our general logistics services

What areas in Africa do you cover for logistics services? +

We currently operate in 12+ African countries including Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Cte d'Ivoire, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Senegal, Ethiopia, Zambia, and Mozambique. Our network is continuously expanding to cover more regions across the continent.

How do you ensure the safety and security of shipments? +

We implement multiple security measures including GPS tracking for all vehicles, secure warehousing with 24/7 surveillance, tamper-evident packaging, and trained security personnel. All high-value shipments receive additional security protocols and insurance coverage.

What is your typical delivery timeframe for cross-border shipments? +

Delivery timeframes vary based on destination and service level. Standard cross-border shipments typically take 5-7 business days, express services deliver in 2-4 business days, and priority services offer 24-48 hour delivery. Customs clearance can add 1-3 days depending on the countries involved.

Do you offer temperature-controlled logistics for sensitive goods? +

Yes, we have specialized temperature-controlled logistics solutions for pharmaceuticals, perishable foods, and other sensitive goods. Our fleet includes refrigerated vehicles, and we maintain temperature-controlled warehouses with real-time monitoring systems to ensure product integrity throughout the supply chain.

How can I track my shipment in real-time? +

All shipments come with real-time tracking accessible through our customer dashboard, mobile app, or via SMS/email updates. You'll receive a unique tracking number that allows you to monitor your shipment's location, estimated arrival time, and delivery status 24/7.

What documentation is required for cross-border shipments? +

Required documentation typically includes commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, bill of lading/waybill, and any product-specific certificates or licenses. Our customs brokerage team will guide you through the specific requirements for your shipment's origin and destination countries.
